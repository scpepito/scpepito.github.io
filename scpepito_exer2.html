Previous Projects

CMSC 22 Group Project

Our group project, titled "Icy Engineers," is a real-time strategy multiplayer game designed to provide an exhilarating player-vs-player experience. In this pixel art-styled game, players battle to control a central point of interest on the map where they aim to build three snowmen to win. The game integrates strategic elements such as snowball fights where players can hinder their opponents, and various power-ups that affect abilities temporarily, adding layers of tactical depth to each match. This engaging and competitive setup promises to deliver a unique and enjoyable gaming experience.

CMSC 23 My Mini Project

This program is a Flutter-based application designed to manage user profiles and their associated friends and slambook entries. It allows users to sign in, view, and edit their profile details, change profile pictures, as well as add, update, or delete friends and their slambook entries. The app integrates Firebase for secure authentication and data management, ensuring users can effectively manage their social circle and personal information in a user-friendly interface.
